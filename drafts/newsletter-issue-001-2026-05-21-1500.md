**Big Idea:** The highest‑margin digital content in 2026 isn’t chasing the newest format — it’s pairing a ultra‑specific, low‑friction product (like a Notion template) with a targeted, short‑form newsletter sequence that teaches the user how to get maximum value. This combo raises perceived value, reduces support load, and creates a repeatable revenue stream.  

**Why it works (evidence):**  
- The SEO brief shows “best notion templates for productivity 2026” draws ~1,050 monthly searches with medium competition (T2 EXTRACTED).

- Market intelligence notes rising demand for template‑based productivity aids and micro‑learning emails (T3 INFERRED).  
- Bundling a template with a 5‑email mini‑course lifts perceived value and conversion (T3 INFERRED).  

**Actionable Intelligence (use today):**  
1. Open your existing content library.  
2. Identify ONE template, checklist, or swipe file you already own.  
3. Draft a five‑email mini‑course that walks a user from setup to advanced use‑case (each email ≤150 words, one action per email).  
4. Offer the template + course as a single product at a 20‑30% premium over the template alone.
