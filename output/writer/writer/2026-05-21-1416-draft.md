## How to Customize These Bundles Without Breaking Them  

Even the best template needs a personal touch. Follow this three‑step workflow to make any bundle truly yours:  

1. **Audit the structure** – Open the sidebar and note which databases are linked (look for the “linked database” icon). Changing a property in the source will reflect everywhere, so edit source tables first.  
2. **Adjust views, not tables** – Create new views (Table, Board, Calendar, Gallery) to suit your preferred workflow. Keep the original view as a backup in case you need to revert.  
3. **Leverage template buttons & formulas** – Use Notion’s built‑in template buttons to generate recurring items (e.g., a weekly review page). Tweak formulas gradually—test with a dummy entry before applying to live data.  

*Pro tip:* Duplicate the entire bundle into a “Sandbox” workspace before making major changes. This way you can experiment safely and always roll back to the original version.
